📍 2-Day Arches National Park Adventure: Built Around Sunrises and Scenery

Destination: Moab, Utah

Duration: 2 days + arrival evening

Designed for: A solo trip with remote work, balanced between adventure and recharge

Trip type: #NationalPark #EarlyHiking #SoloFriendly #ScenicViews

🎯 Trip Goals

  • Catch Arches’ most iconic trails at sunrise, before heat and crowds
  • Fit in scenic highlights without overpacking the schedule
  • Weave in remote work hours (Eastern Time) without stress
  • Keep afternoons light, prioritizing drive-up viewpoints and shaded trails
  • Maximize park variety with a Canyonlands sunset finale

⚠️ Planning Considerations

  • Scheduled all major hikes before sunrise for cooler temps and golden hour light
  • Built in generous downtime for work, meals, and recovery
  • Structured each day around Eastern Time work hours, with buffer before sunset
  • Prioritized short trails and scenic drives for heat avoidance
  • Researched optional add-ons for flexibility based on weather and energy

🧠 Trip Design Strategy

This two-day Arches itinerary was built around two key constraints: summer desert heat and Eastern Time remote work hours. Mornings were anchored by major hikes — The Windows Section and Delicate Arch — timed for the softest light and fewest crowds. Between late morning and evening, I stayed indoors for work and rest, with optional scenic stops researched in case of extra energy or favorable weather.

  • Sunrise strategy: Prioritized early hikes to beat the heat and crowds
  • Work integration: Midday downtime aligned with Eastern Time remote work hours
  • Evening flexibility: Built-in time for scenic drives or rest before sunset outings
  • Fresh finale: Added Canyonlands for variety and a no-repeat route

Each moment was designed to feel relaxed yet rewarding — a steady rhythm of exploration, comfort, and awe.

🗺️ Final Route

📍 Day 0 – Arrival in Moab

  • Arrive and check into lodging (10 min from park entrance)
  • Pick up groceries or grab a light dinner
  • Early rest to prep for sunrise hike

📍 Day 1 – Sunrise Hike & Scenic Evening

☀️ Early Morning (4:30–7:00 AM)

  • The Windows Section — North & South Windows and Double Arch
  • Return for breakfast, shower, and recharge (~30 min)

💻 Midday (7:00 AM–3:00 PM)

  • Remote work block (Eastern Time)
  • Optional: casual lunch or nearby town biking

🚗 Afternoon Viewpoints (4:30–7:00 PM)

  • Sand Dune Arch — shaded, short, and peaceful
  • Scenic drive past Balanced Rock and other overlooks
  • Optional: Broken Arch Loop or Fiery Furnace Viewpoint

🌄 Sunset (8:00–9:00 PM)

  • Panorama Point — wide views, glows even with partial clouds
  • Optional: La Sal Mountain Viewpoint for sunset glow if skies are clear

📍 Day 2 – Delicate Arch & Canyonlands Sunset

☀️ Early Morning (4:00–7:00 AM)

  • Delicate Arch Trail — iconic at dawn
  • Optional: Park Avenue Trail — short & photogenic
  • Return for breakfast, shower, and recharge (~30 min)

💻 Midday (7:00 AM–3:00 PM)

  • Remote work block
  • Lunch, pack up, rest before sunset

🌄 Evening (6:00–9:30 PM)

  • Drive to Canyonlands National Park (Island in the Sky)
  • Viewpoints: Mesa Arch, Candlestick Tower, Buck Canyon Overlook
  • Short trail at Grand View Point Overlook (~7:30 PM)
  • Sunset at Orange Cliff Overlook (~8:30 PM)
